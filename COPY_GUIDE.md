## Content Guidelines

### Tone & Voice
- **Professional but approachable** - We're experts, but we speak in plain language
- **Action-oriented** - Focus on outcomes and results
- **Future-focused** - Emphasize 2026 trends and beyond
- **Value-driven** - Always highlight business benefits

### SEO Best Practices
- Use target keywords naturally
- Include H1, H2, H3 structure
- Add meta descriptions (150-160 characters)
- Include internal links
- Optimize images with alt text

### Conversion Optimization
- Clear CTAs on every page
- Social proof (testimonials, stats)
- Risk reduction (guarantees, free trials)
- Urgency where appropriate
- Multiple contact methods
